Once you are done editing your app, and you have linked it to a deployment using its API endpoint, you can download the view_comfy.json file from the editor.

You then need to go to the ViewComfy Apps tab on the dashboard and click "Deploy App".

![Deploy a ViewComfy app](/public/deploy_a_viewcomfy_app.webp)

From there, you just need to give it a name and drop your view_comfy.json. 

[This](https://youtu.be/HDerbVckuno) video goes over this process.
